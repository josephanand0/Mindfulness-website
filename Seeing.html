Mindful Seeing

What is Mindful Seeing?

Mindful seeing is the practice of consciously noticing everything within your visual field, Mercree tells Elite Daily over email. You do this to focus completely on [one thing] as much as you are able. It takes your mind from a place of thinking and doing to a place of noticing.

MEDITATION FOR MINDFUL BREATHING

In this exercise, people pay close attention to the experience of seeing. Before sitting down to listen to this meditation, you will need to find a small stone for people to use as their “focus rock.” You might make a fun outing of going on a walk to find a special rock.
